\subsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxyref{Service\+Container}{p.}{class_cookie_eng_1_1_services_1_1_service_container} acts as a container for services that can be located by the \doxyref{Service\+Locator}{p.}{class_cookie_eng_1_1_services_1_1_service_locator}. 

This class works to allocate pointers to the various services and handle their deletion once the container is shut down.
